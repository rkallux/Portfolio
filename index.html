5-Stage Multicore Pipelined RISC-V Processor

Verilog · Quartus · FPGA

Four-core, five-stage pipelined RISC-V processor with hazard detection, squashing, and stalling across 34+ ISA instructions. Includes a 4 KB, 2-way set-associative write-allocate cache with LRU replacement, and a comparison of bimodal vs. global branch predictors.

8-Bit Sigmoid via ROM Lookup & Linear Interpolation

Cadence Virtuoso · CMOS · MATLAB

Transistor-level 8-bit digital circuit computing the sigmoid activation function. A 32-entry NOR ROM stores base values f(x) and 5-bit first-difference deltas, addressed by the upper five bits of the input; a 5×3 Wallace-tree multiplier scales the delta by the lower three fractional bits, and an 8-bit Sklansky adder produces the interpolated output.

Three non-overlapping clock phases (precharge / evaluate / present) separate bitline conditioning from combinational evaluation. Full-adder, half-adder, and ROM cells were designed at the transistor level. Verified bit-exactly across all 256 inputs against a golden MATLAB model.
